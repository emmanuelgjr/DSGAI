Three Regulatory Failure Patterns

① Unlawful Basis & Consent Failure

UPSTREAM FAILURE
Training data collected without documented lawful basis
↓
Consent never captured or since expired
↓
Processing purpose not disclosed at collection
↓
Data already ingested into training pipeline — processing cannot be unwound
↓
Data subject exercises rights → no lawful basis can be demonstrated
⚠ GDPR Art. 5 & 6 · CCPA opt-out · HIPAA minimum necessary

② The Erasure Gap

GDPR ART. 17
GDPR erasure request received
↓
Raw record deleted from CRM & data lake ✓
↓
Embedding persists in vector store ✗
↓
Fine-tuned weights encode deleted record ✗
↓
Model continues surfacing deleted content in production — erasure structurally incomplete
⚠ Machine unlearning cannot fully remediate — GenAI-specific compliance gap

③ Lineage Absence at Obligation Point

ARCHITECTURE FAILURE

↓

↓
No consent records mapped to specific training records
↓
Cannot scope blast radius of compromised data source
↓
Cannot prove compliance · Cannot scope remediation · Cannot execute targeted retraining
⚠ Not a legal problem — a technical architecture failure that blocks legal remedy
