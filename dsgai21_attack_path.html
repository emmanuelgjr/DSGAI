DSGAI21 · Attack Path

Disinformation & Integrity Attacks via Data Poisoning

False data injected into trusted sources — surfaced by AI as authoritative, grounded, cited output

Primary Attack Flow
🎯 Adversary Seeds False Data
Wiki, threat intel feed, open corpus, or RAG knowledge store
→
Trusted Source Ingested
No anomaly detection — false records achieve high semantic relevance
→
RAG Pipeline Retrieves Poisoned Record
High semantic match — surfaced with full authority of retrieval system
→
AI Presents Disinformation as Fact
Cited, grounded output — no hallucination occurred
→
💥 Operational Damage
Incorrect patch deployed · wrong clinical decision · false risk score
The Integrity Attack — Two Surfaces, One Core Failure
⚠ THE MODEL DID NOT HALLUCINATE — IT ACCURATELY RETRIEVED AND PRESENTED WHAT IT WAS GIVEN  ·  THE FAILURE WAS ENTIRELY IN THE DATA
🔴 Attack Surface 1 — Training Time
Malicious contributors inject misleading samples into open / crowdsourced / scraped dataset
↓
Subtly altered facts · fabricated citations · poisoned embeddings at low frequency
↓
Base or fine-tuned model internalizes false beliefs
↓
Technically challenging to remove without full retraining — persists through deployment
🟣 Attack Surface 2 — Retrieval Time
Adversary writes false records to trusted source: internal wiki · threat intel feed · news aggregator
↓
False records retrieved with high semantic relevance — no trust-tier differentiation
↓
Surfaced to users with implicit authority of grounded, cited AI output
↓
Propagates into automated decisions before human verification step
CRISIS AMPLIFICATION
During active incident response, zero-day cycles, or breaking threat intel — operators query AI rapidly under pressure with reduced capacity for source verification. An adversary seeding false technical details (incorrect CVE descriptions, fabricated patch status, misleading IOCs) into near-real-time RAG sources causes maximum damage precisely when it is least likely to be challenged. Grok incident: production RAG system ingested and parroted externally introduced false information as authoritative output.
Attack Vectors

① Zero-Day Remediation Poisoning

CRISIS TIMING
Adversary seeds fabricated patch instructions during active zero-day disclosure
↓
Plausible-looking but incorrect remediation posted to threat intel aggregator
↓
Enterprise RAG pipelines index aggregator as trusted source — near real-time
↓
Security assistants retrieve and present false guidance as authoritative with citation
↓
Teams deploy incorrect remediation believing systems are patched — discovered only via second-order incident
⚠ Every system performed exactly as designed — the integrity failure was in the data

② Open Training Corpus Poisoning

TRAINING TIME
Malicious contributors submit poisoned samples to crowdsourced or scraped dataset
↓
Low-frequency injection — altered facts, fabricated citations — passes basic validation
↓
No adversarial integrity evaluation before fine-tuning run
↓
Model internalizes false beliefs — encoded in weights, not in any retrievable document
↓
False claims surface in production — removal requires full retraining · no Dataset Bill of Materials to scope impact
⚠ Even 250 poisoned samples can produce measurable impact (Anthropic research)

③ Internal Wiki Write-Access Compromise

KNOWLEDGE STORE
Attacker gains write access to internal wiki or knowledge base via phished credential
↓
Inserts subtly false policy guidance, process documentation, or technical specs
↓
RAG pipeline indexes wiki as high-trust internal source — no write-access audit trail
↓
AI assistant surfaces false policy as authoritative to employees and automated workflows
↓
Compliance or operational decisions made on false internal guidance — write access was treated as data management, not security boundary
⚠ Write access to RAG-indexed sources = write access to production AI output
Resulting Impact
🧠 False Beliefs Encoded in Weights
Persist through deployment · resist targeted removal
📡 Authoritative Disinformation
Cited, grounded AI output — carries implicit retrieval authority
⚡ Operational Damage at Crisis Timing
Wrong patch deployed · false IOCs · incorrect clinical decision
🔗 Automated Pipeline Propagation
No human verification step — disinformation reaches decisions directly
