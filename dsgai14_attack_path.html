Attack Vectors

① Debug Mode Left Enabled

HYGIENE FAILURE
Engineers enable debug mode to investigate flaky agent
↓
Full HTTP bodies logged — OAuth tokens, system prompts, customer data
↓
Debug mode never reverted — no automatic expiry configured
↓
Months of sensitive data accumulate in logging cluster
↓
Phished SOC analyst account — attacker exports entire debug log archive
⚠ Logs outlive and out-scope primary systems — retroactive privacy exposure

② Third-Party APM / Analytics Vendor

THIRD-PARTY RISK
Team integrates external log analytics platform (e.g. Mixpanel)
↓
Full prompt & response payloads forwarded to vendor — no DPA reviewed
↓
Vendor held to lower security standard than primary data stores
↓
Vendor suffers breach or misconfigures data access
↓
Conversation history, PII, and secrets exposed via third-party platform
⚠ Ref: OpenAI / Mixpanel incident Nov 2025 — even limited log data is sensitive

③ Mis-Scoped SIEM Search Permissions

ACCESS CONTROL
Observability platform grants broad search to analyst role
↓
No restriction on cross-session, cross-user, or bulk export queries
↓
Insider or compromised account queries: "oauth_token" across all logs
↓
Thousands of live credentials returned in single search — no alert
↓
Credentials replayed — lateral access to production systems and data stores
⚠ Observability platform becomes easier exfiltration path than primary data stores
